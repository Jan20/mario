The Game

The goal of each level is to reach its end as quickly as possible while trying to collect as many coins as possible. While progressing through the levels you will encounter a ranch of opponents. Either avoid them by jumping over them or you can destroy them by jumping right on top of them.

Controls

To control your avatar, use the following keys:

arrow_forward
Right arrow Key: Move to the right
arrow_backward
Left arrow key: Move to the left
arrow_upward
Up Key: Jump
space_bar
Space: Shoot a fireball (only available after collecting a power-up)
