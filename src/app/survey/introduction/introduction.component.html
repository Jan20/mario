Introduction Online Experiment

Ajusting the difficutly to a dirrent playing styles poses an important challenge in designing video games. The experiment consists of a Jump-and-Run game similar to Super Mario Bros and a short questionare after every level. Please feel free to play as many levels as you want and answer a small questionare attached after finshing a level.

The Game

The goal of each level is to reach its end as quickly as possible while trying to collect as many coins as possible.

Controls

To control your avatar, use the following keys:

arrow_forward
Right arrow Key: Move to the right
arrow_backward
Left arrow key: Move to the left
arrow_upward
Up Key: Jump
space_bar
Space: Shoot a fireball (only available after collecting fireball-power-up)
